Jonathan Goldstein

Fine Artist

Jonathan Goldstein is an American fine artist and amateur photographer from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. He received his BA from Temple University in 2013. His artistic process began while abroad in Italy where he studied Roman history including Italian painting and sculpture from the 13th through the early 16th centuries. Weekly class lectures and on-site visits exposed him to breathtaking art found in and around the "Eternal City." This time abroad inspired him to pick up photography and venture out into other mediums. The pleasure and fun of creating is the driving force behind his art. His art is characterized by vibrant colors, contrasting shapes, and bold compositions.
